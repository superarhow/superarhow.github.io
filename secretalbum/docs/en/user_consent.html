User Consent & Advertising

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This page explains how SecretAlbum Lite uses advertising and your choices regarding data collection. SecretAlbum Premium does not display advertisements and this page does not apply to it.

Note (GDPR / EEA & UK users): Under GDPR and UK GDPR, we are required to obtain your consent before serving personalized advertisements. You may choose non-personalized ads at any time.

1. Advertising in SecretAlbum Lite

SecretAlbum Lite uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers – Advertising ID (GAID)
  • Approximate location – country/region from IP address
  • App interaction data – ad impressions and clicks
  • Device information – OS version, screen size, language

2. Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

Personalized ads are tailored to your interests based on data collected across apps and websites. This requires your consent in the EEA and UK.

Non-personalized ads are based only on contextual information and do not involve profiling.

Manage ad personalization: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization

3. Your Rights

  • Consent / Withdrawal – Grant or withdraw consent via device settings at any time.
  • Right to access – See Google Account Data & Privacy.
  • Right to erasure – Reset or delete your Advertising ID via device Google settings.
  • CCPA (California) – We do not sell your personal data. Opt out via device Advertising ID settings.
